# Liftoff X — Funding & Cost Expectations

> Honest, decision-oriented view of **when** to raise outside money and **how much**
> each route costs. Figures are **industry-typical estimates / ranges — not quotes.**
> The real numbers come out of the Phase 2 operator/aggregator calls (see
> [`MASTER-PLAN.md`](./MASTER-PLAN.md) and [`OPERATOR-CALL-QUESTIONS.md`](./OPERATOR-CALL-QUESTIONS.md)).

---

## TL;DR
- **Don't raise from outside investors yet.** You have a product + sales kit but **no
  market validation** (no operator/aggregator commitment). Raising now = low
  valuation = giving away too much equity for too little money.
- **Sequence:** finish the cheap **outreach (Phase 2)** → land 2–3 interested
  parties or a **Letter of Intent** → *then* raise (validation lifts your
  valuation and cuts dilution).
- **How much you need depends entirely on the route** (below). The JV route needs
  little/no upfront capital; the own-studio route is the only one that needs a
  real raise (~€150k–400k).

> One LOI from an aggregator does more for your valuation than the whole app does today.

---

## When to raise

| Stage | Do you need investors? |
|---|---|
| **Now → validation** | **No.** Costs are time + a few hundred euro. Bootstrap it. |
| **After 2–3 LOIs / clear demand** | **Maybe** — and now on *good* terms. |
| **Committing to own studio (cert + licence)** | **Yes** — this is the real raise. |

Raising *before* validation is the classic mistake: you sell equity cheap to fund
work you could de-risk for free first.

---

## Cost by route

### A — Now → validation (bootstrap, near-free)
| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Domain + Render upgrade (no cold-start during demos) | ~€20–40 / month |
| One-pager + deck to PDF, light design polish | €0–500 |
| Outreach (LinkedIn, email, calls) | your time |

**No investor required.** This phase is intentionally cheap — it exists to buy
*answers*, not infrastructure.

### B — JV / "Powered By" an already-licensed studio (fast, capital-light)
- Often **€0 upfront** — you trade a share of revenue instead of paying for
  certification and licences.
- Partner brings the **licence, certification, and aggregator connections**.
- "Cost" = a lower rev-share to you, **not** cash up front.
- Examples of this model in the market: Relax "Powered By", Yggdrasil YG Masters.
- **Little/no external capital needed** → likely no big investor needed either.

### C — Own studio (your own licence + certification) — Phase 5, capital-heavy
The only route that genuinely needs investment. Rough, **industry-typical** ranges:

| Item | Indicative range |
|---|---|
| RNG certification (GLI-19 / GLI-11 or iTech), per market | ~€15k–50k |
| B2B supplier licence (Curaçao / Anjouan cheaper → MGA pricier) | ~€10k–60k setup + annual |
| RGS made production-ready (wallet API, Redis, audit log, hardening) | ~€30k–100k (contractors) or months of your own dev |
| Legal, compliance, contingency buffer | ~€20k–50k |
| **Realistic total** | **~€150k–400k over 6–18 months** |

This is the number you'd raise for — **but only after Phase 2/Phase 4.**

---

## Recommendation
1. **No outside investor right now.** You don't need one and the timing hurts your
   valuation.
2. Run the **outreach**; let the market tell you which route (B or C) is realistic
   and what cert/licence *actually* cost in your target market — those real numbers
   replace the estimates above.
3. Then choose deliberately:
   - **Route B (JV):** little/no capital → probably no raise.
   - **Route C (own studio):** raise **€150k–400k**, with validation in hand so you
     get favourable terms.

> ⚠️ Until certification + licensing are done, Liftoff X is **provably fair but not
> certified/licensed** — never claim otherwise (see the disclaimer in
> [`MATH-RTP-VOLATILITY.md`](./MATH-RTP-VOLATILITY.md)).
