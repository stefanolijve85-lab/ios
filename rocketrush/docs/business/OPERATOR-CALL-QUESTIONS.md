# LiftoffX — Operator / Aggregator Call Questions

> Step 4 of the plan: turn 5–10 sales calls into **market research**. Go in to
> *learn*, not just to sell. The answers here directly feed:
> `OPERATOR-ECONOMICS.md` (assumptions) and the licensing/JV decision (step 5).
>
> Tip: don't fire all of these — pick the 6–8 that matter for each contact, and
> always end with "what would make you say yes?".

---

## 0. Frame the call (first 2 minutes)
- One-liner: *"Aviator-class crash game, with a social/retention layer and a
  better revenue model for you. I'd love your read on fit and on a couple of
  numbers."*
- Send the **live demo link** before or at the start.

## 1. Commercials (feeds the economics model)
- What **rev-share band** is standard for crash/instant content right now?
- Is it **flat or volume-tiered**? What tiers do you see?
- Flat **monthly fee** ever preferred over rev-share? At what volume?
- For content via an aggregator, what's the **aggregator's cut** — a % of the
  provider's share, or a markup added to us/the operator?
- Any **upfront / integration / certification fees** typically expected?

## 2. The real player numbers (replaces our guesses)
- Typical **average bet size** on crash games in your markets?
- **Rounds per active player per session/month** you tend to see?
- Rough **active-player counts** a mid-tier operator runs on a hit crash game?
- What **GGR / RTP hold** do crash games actually deliver for you?

## 3. Markets & product fit
- Which **markets/geographies** convert best for crash games (LatAm, India,
  Africa, SEA, EU…)?
- Which **RTP versions** do you need (97 / 96 / 95)? Different per market?
- Any features you'd need **on or off** by jurisdiction (chat, social feed,
  leaderboards, max-win cap)?
- Appetite for a **white-label / branded** version? Would you pay a premium for it?
- Is our **social/retention layer** (leaderboard, activity feed, profiles) a real
  differentiator to you, or a nice-to-have?

## 4. Integration & tech (feeds RGS-ARCHITECTURE.md)
- Which **wallet API** do you use — seamless/transactional, single wallet, or your
  own? Can you share the spec?
- Do you integrate providers **direct** or only via **aggregators**? Which
  aggregators are you already on?
- What's your typical **integration timeline** for a new game?
- Requirements around **idempotency, rollback, reconciliation, reporting**?
- **Responsible-gaming** hooks you require pass-through for?

## 5. Certification & licensing (feeds the step-5 decision)
- Which **certifications** must we hold to be listed (GLI-19/GLI-11, iTech, BMM)?
  Per market?
- Which **supplier licence(s)** do you require (MGA, UKGC, Curaçao/CGA, Anjouan…)?
- Would you onboard a game from a provider that is **certified but not yet fully
  licensed in every market**, starting with lighter-touch jurisdictions?
- Do you ever work with new studios via an **already-licensed partner / white-label
  provider** (the JV shortcut)?

## 6. Decision & next steps
- Who's in the **buying decision** (BD, product, compliance)?
- What's your **content onboarding pipeline / cadence** look like?
- **"What would make you say yes?"** — the single most useful question.
- Agree a concrete next step (intro to their integration team / a follow-up with
  the math sheet / a slot in their onboarding queue).
