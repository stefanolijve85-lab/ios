## 2. Outreach sequence (per target)

1. **LinkedIn connect** with a 1-line note (template C).
2. **Email** the BD/partnerships contact or submit the partner form (template A).
3. **Follow-up** after 4–5 working days if no reply (template B).
4. **20-min call** → use the demo script (§4) + question list.
5. **Log it** in the capture sheet in `OPERATOR-CALL-QUESTIONS.md`.

Keep it to ~3 touches per contact, then move on. Quality > volume — 5–10 good
conversations is the goal.

## 4. Demo-call script (≈5 minutes)

**Goal of the call:** half pitch, half *research* — learn fit, certs, wallet, and
market-standard rev-share (feeds the economics model).

| Time | Do / say |
|---|---|
| 0:00 | **Frame:** "Aviator-class crash + a social layer + better operator economics. I'll show it live in 2 min, then I'd love your read on fit and what you'd need from us." |
| 0:30 | **Demo live** (share www.liftoffx.com): place **two bets**, watch the rocket, **cash out one early, ride the other**, hit the **win pop-up**. Call out: **provably fair** (tap to verify), **live players**, **leaderboard + activity feed** (the retention layer). |
| 2:30 | **The pitch:** configurable RTP, **volume-tiered rev-share (operators keep more)**, white-label/"powered by operator". "We don't just split the pie cheaper — the social layer grows it." |
| 3:30 | **Ask + learn** (use the question list): "Does this fit your hub? What **certs/wallet** would you need? What **rev-share** is standard for crash content?" |
| 4:30 | **Next step:** agree an intro to their integration team, or a follow-up with the **math/RTP sheet**. |

### Objection handling
- **"We already have Aviator/Spribe."** → Exactly why this is interesting: operators
  want a **second** crash option to diversify supplier risk and keep more margin.
  We're the differentiated alternative, not a clone.
- **"Are you certified / licensed?"** → Provably fair today; clear certification
  roadmap (GLI/iTech). We'd **prioritise the markets and certs you need**, and
  we're open to a Powered-By / white-label route to shortcut it.
- **"What's your traction?"** → MVP is **live with real multiplayer + social**.
  We're selecting **launch partners now** — early partners get input on the
  roadmap and preferential terms.
- **"Why would players switch?"** → They don't switch *games* — the **social layer**
  (leaderboards, activity, tournaments) increases session length and retention vs.
  a plain crash game, which is upside for the operator at the same hold.
- **"Rev-share?"** → Volume-tiered (e.g. 14% → 10% → 7%); flat-fee option at scale.
  "Tell me what's market for you and we'll make it work."
