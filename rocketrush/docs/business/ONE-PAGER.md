### Why it's different
- **Configurable RTP** — tune house edge to each market (incumbents ship fixed RTP).
- **Better, volume-tiered rev-share** — operators keep more margin (see commercials).
- **Social/retention layer built in** — live leaderboard, activity feed, public
  profiles, chat → more rounds per player → bigger GGR for the operator.
- **White-label / "powered by operator"** skinning — own the brand, not us.
