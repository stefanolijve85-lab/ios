# 19 — Future Expansion Ideas (Year 2+)

## A. Modes

### A1. Co-op "Doubles"
Two players co-launch in sequence; second player keeps combo of the first if launched within timing window. Highly social, GIF-worthy.

### A2. PvP "Mirror Match"
Asynchronous PvP: your replay vs another player's replay, side-by-side. Winner = greater distance with same launcher class.

### A3. Daily Tournament
Fixed launcher + projectile, top 100 globally win exclusive cosmetics.

### A4. Endless Mode
True infinite scroll. Procedural generator unlocks at Prestige 10. Difficulty asymptotically caps; leaderboards segmented by 1k m bracket.

### A5. Sandbox Mode (Premium-Unlock)
Unlimited projectiles, no scoring, place obstacles freely. For creators / streamers. Drives social content.

## B. Mechanics

### B1. Charged Abilities
Long-press during flight to charge an ability (e.g., 3x bigger Apple split, super-rocket).

### B2. Element System
Projectiles get elemental traits — fire (chains burn), ice (freezes obstacles for combo), lightning (chain-lightning across obstacles). Each biome has elemental affinities.

### B3. Modular Launchers
Swap launcher parts (frame / band / grip) for stat trade-offs. RPG-style loot lite.

### B4. Run Modifiers
Pre-launch "buff cards": "+50% drag", "double combo decay" — risk/reward.

## C. Content

### C1. New Biomes
- **Pirate Cove** (water bounces, tide cycles).
- **Underwater Reef** (slow physics, magnet-friendly).
- **Volcano** (rising lava, urgency).
- **Cloud Castle** (vertical traversal).
- **Anime School Rooftops** (collab IP slot).

### C2. Projectile Families
- "Mythic" line: dragon egg, phoenix feather.
- "Snack" line: ramen, taco, donut.
- "Brand collab" line: licensed IP only, never gameplay-advantaged.

### C3. Boss Library
Add 1 boss per biome over time. Bosses are *encounters*, not bullet sponges — kill in 5–8 seconds.

### C4. Cinematics
Per-biome 6-sec intro cinematic the first time the player reaches it.

## D. Social

### D1. Clans / Slings (Guilds)
Up to 30 players. Shared weekly challenges. Earn clan-exclusive cosmetics. Cosmetic-only — no power gating.

### D2. Replay Sharing
Auto-export 10-sec clips of PB runs. Native share. Watermarked with "Launched!" branding for viral lift.

### D3. UGC: Custom Biomes
Editor SDK released as a web tool. Curated drops. Creator revenue share via gem tipping.

## E. Monetization Expansion

### E1. VIP Pass (Subscription, $4.99/mo)
- Permanent +25% coin gain.
- Daily VIP crate.
- Exclusive vanity badge.
- Skip first ad each session.
**Hard rule:** no gameplay power above what's purchasable as one-off.

### E2. Cosmetic Shop Tabs
Featured rotating shelves curated by theme. Personalized recommendations powered by ML.

### E3. Brand Collabs
2 per year, premium SKUs only. Examples: a film tie-in skin, a snack brand projectile.

## F. Platform

### F1. Apple Vision / Mixed Reality
Mini-game version: room-scale slingshot with hand tracking. Sold as a $4.99 upgrade.

### F2. Steam / Tablet
Premium $9.99 SKU stripped of ads. Same content, no IAP. Targets dad/family audience.

### F3. TV
Apple TV / Fire TV with controller. "Family living room" pivot.

### F4. Web (HTML5)
Reduced-feature browser version as a UA top-of-funnel.

## G. Technology

### G1. Replay Validation Service
Backend service validates top 1% submissions for leaderboard integrity. Anti-cheat.

### G2. ML-Driven Difficulty
Per-player obstacle density tuned dynamically to keep flow-state.

### G3. Generative Cosmetic Skins
Designer-controlled GenAI tool to scale cosmetic output (always reviewed by humans).

## H. IP Universe

- Animated short series on YouTube: 3-min episodes starring Pip the tennis ball.
- Plush toy line if MAU > 5M.
- Boardgame adaptation.
- Comic book series via Webtoons.

## I. Sustainability

- Carbon-aware server scheduling for backend.
- Charity skin lines tied to nonprofits (proceeds split disclosed in-app).

## Roadmap Cadence

Year 2: 4 biomes, 12 projectiles, 1 co-op mode, VIP launched.
Year 3: PvP, UGC SDK, brand collab #2, console softlaunch.
Year 5: stable franchise — sustained 1M DAU target.

## Killed Ideas (Lessons Learned)

- **Energy/lives system** — anti-retention, never revisit.
- **Loot box gambling-style spend escalators** — incompatible with our ethical pillar.
- **Hard-paywalled levels** — same.
- **Mandatory account creation** — auth optional, never blocking play.
